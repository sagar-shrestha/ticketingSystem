Shristi - A voting platform for Nepal Idol Season 4 for those who wants to vote from abroad.

Nepal Idol is a Nepali reality television singing competition that is part of the Idols franchise created by Simon Fuller and owned by Fremantle Media. The judges are Sugam Pokhrel, Subani Moktan and Sambhujeet Baskota. Nepal Idol Season 4 was officially announced on July 13 by AP1 TV by uploading its promotional video in YouTube. The other announced locations for the auditions were Bhairahawa, Butwal, Nepalgunj, Pokhara, Birtamod and Kathmandu.
